Tiropitas

Greek cheese rolls

Crispy filo dough with a cheesy filling makes the perfect summer snack or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 lb cottage cheese, small curd
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 lb filo dough sheets, thawed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Combine feta cheese, cottage cheese, eggs and salt in a bowl. Mix well.
  3. Lay out filo dough horizontally and cut into thirds, stick in a large ziploc bag to keep from drying out.
  4. On a large cutting board lay out 3 strips of filo dough, brush lightly with melted butter and add small spoonfull of cheese mixture along the bottom edge of each strip.
  5. Carefully roll the filo dough, starting at the bottom, until you get to the end of the strip.
  6. Place rolls in pan and brush the tops with melted butter. Repeat the process until you've used up all of your filling.
  7. Bake the tiropitas for about 20 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool before serving.
