Best Grilled Chicken Seasoning

Grilled chicken legs with the best seasoning

This seasoing blend is so easy and makes the most delicious grilled chicken legs.

Ingredients

  • 3-4lbs chicken legs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil, or oil of choice
  • 2 tbs lemon pepper seasoing
  • 2 tbs garlic and herb seasoning

Directions

  1. In a small bowl combine lemon pepper with the garlic and herb seasoning. Set aside.
  2. Using paper towels, dry off the chicken legs as much as possible.
  3. In a large bowl combine chicken legs and oil, toss until the chicken is well covered.
  4. Once chicken is coated in oil sprinkle in about half of the seasoing mix, toss to coat the chicken. Repeat with remaining seasoing, rubbing the seasoing under the skin.
  5. While chicken sits, preheat the grill to 400 degrees. Once preheated turn the heat down to low.
  6. Place chicken on grill, making sure not to overcrowd, and cook, turning once or twice, until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
  7. Remove from grill and cover with foil, let chicken stand for 5 minutes before serving.
