Greek Salad

Yummy salad with mediterranean flavors

Cucumber, tomatoes and red onions in an olive oil and red wine vinegar based dressing. Perfect paired with grilled chicken, tzazkiki and pita bread for a delicious dinner!

Ingredients

For Salad
  • 1 english cucumber, diced
  • 5 small tomatoes, deseeded and diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • optional: feta cheese and kalamta olives
For Dressing
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbl red wine vinegar
  • juice from half a lemon
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tbs fresh basil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Combine ingredients for dressing in large container with lid. Stir to combine.
  2. dice vegetables and add to container with dressing. Add an additional ingredients if using
  3. Refrigerate for at least an hour to chill and let flavors combine. Stays good in the refrigerator for at least 3 days. If it lasts that long!
